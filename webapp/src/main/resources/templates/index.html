Your personal Lyrics Analyzer

Instructions

Lyra is an open lyrics analyzer tool. After you uploaded your Excel file you can analyze the given data in different ways. You got a searchsection that allows you to search for terms or phrases in your lyrics, song titles, artist names and so on. Another section is the map area. Here you will see all the places mentioned in the lyrics and the biographical dates of the different artists you entered in your data table. All these places are detected and localized automatically.

In the last area you can find tools concerning the weighted word frequencies. So you can either compare the important terms used by an artist with those of another artist or compare phases of one artist.
