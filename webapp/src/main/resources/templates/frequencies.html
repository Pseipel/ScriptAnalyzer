This section enables you to detect relevant words of an artist in a chosen period. In this case, relevant means that the words appear more often in this period than in the remaining corpus. Furthermore, you can compare the detected words to the words of another period and/or another artist.
With "Count" you can define how many words you want to get back. If you choose "Compilations" the result includes lyrics from compilations, too. If you are only interested in the original releases you leave this field unchecked. The result contains the most relevant words for the chosen period and artist and their absolute frequencies.
